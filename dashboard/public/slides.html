The x402 Marketplace — Stripe for AI Agents on Casper

Self-serve · Pay-per-call · On-chain settlement
The Problem
$1.7T

the global trade-finance gap — but the bigger story is the machine-to-machine economy that has no payment rail.

AI agents need to pay each other for services — translation, inference, data lookups, trading. Today there is no standard, HTTP-native way for one agent to pay another.

The Solution

A self-serve marketplace
where any agent lists & charges

List a service → price it per-call in CSPR → get paid automatically via x402, settled on Casper.

The Ecosystem Play

“$100k of $150k in prize is x402 credits.
PayMesh is the infrastructure that makes every other x402 project work.”

Other teams build consumers — we build the rails. A marketplace, registry, staking & reputation layer means every x402 consumer agent has somewhere to discover, price, and trust services. This is exactly Casper's judging criteria on long-term impact & ecosystem growth.
